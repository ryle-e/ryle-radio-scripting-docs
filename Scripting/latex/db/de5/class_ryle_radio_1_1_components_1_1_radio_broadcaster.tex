The power of this broadcaster at the maximum and minimum radii. 

The x-\/value is the broadcast power right on the outer radius, and the y-\/value is the broadcast power within the inner radius. Broadcast power between the inner and outer radii will be somewhere in this range, defined by the \doxylink{class_ryle_radio_1_1_components_1_1_radio_broadcaster_af016c4c46d5e76046f83ae9a7162cf78}{distance\+Falloff}

If the x-\/value is greater than 0, you might want to enable \doxylink{class_ryle_radio_1_1_components_1_1_radio_broadcaster_a958c908a813dd76352af07aa0f6a4085}{apply\+To\+All\+Outputs\+Outside} so that all Outputs are affected by this broadcaster.
